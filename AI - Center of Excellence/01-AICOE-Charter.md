# AI Center of Excellence (AICOE) Charter

Use this charter as a template for your organization. Customize the language, roles, and operating model to fit your structure, priorities, and risk profile.

## Purpose

Establish a cross-functional AI Center of Excellence to accelerate responsible AI adoption, build organizational capability, and deliver measurable business value through AI-enabled solutions.

## Scope

- AI strategy, governance, and policy
- Tool enablement (GitHub Copilot, AI-assisted workflows)
- Use-case identification, prioritization, and delivery
- Training, community building, and knowledge sharing
- Measurement and continuous improvement

## Guiding Principles

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Responsible by default** | Every AI initiative follows responsible AI guidelines covering fairness, transparency, privacy, and security. |
| **Practical over theoretical** | Focus on delivering working solutions, not just research. |
| **Enable, don't gatekeep** | Remove friction for teams while maintaining guardrails. |
| **Measure what matters** | Track adoption, productivity, quality, and risk — not vanity metrics. |
| **Iterate fast** | Start small, learn, scale what works. |

## Governance

- **Executive Sponsor**: Accountable for strategy and investment decisions.
- **AICOE Lead**: Responsible for day-to-day operations, roadmap, and stakeholder management.
- **Governance Board**: Reviews high-risk use cases, policy changes, and escalations (meets monthly).
- **Working Groups**: Domain-specific teams (e.g., engineering, data, security) that execute initiatives.

```
└────────────┴────────────┴────────────┴──────────┘
```

### Key Activities

1. **Intake**: Your teams submit AI use cases via a standard intake form.
2. **Triage**: Your AICOE assesses feasibility, risk, and value.
3. **Enable**: Provide tools, training, and support.
4. **Deliver**: Support implementation with patterns, reviews, and guidance.
5. **Measure**: Track outcomes and feed learnings back into the cycle.

## Success Metrics

- Number of teams actively using AI tools
- Time-to-value for new AI use cases
- Developer satisfaction and productivity gains
- Incidents or policy violations (target: zero)
- Use-case pipeline growth quarter over quarter

## Review Cadence

- **Weekly**: AICOE working session
- **Monthly**: Governance board review
- **Quarterly**: Executive update and roadmap refresh
